"Seychelles shows that size is no barrier to the scale of achievements: PM\nSeychelles is a strategic partner for India, says PM\nWe have excellent security cooperation, specially for maritime security in Indian Ocean Region: PM\nAir Services Agreement between India-Seychelles to enable more and easier connections, says the PM\nPM Narendra Modi appreciates Seychelles' support for India's candidature for permanent membership of expanded United Nations Security Council\nIndia and Seychelles vary in size but ties between both countries are a outstanding model of international partnership: PM\nHis Excellency, President Michel,\nMembers of the Media,\nIt is always a great pleasure to welcome home a special friend, which is definitely President Michel.\nI am also happy to reciprocate President Michel's exceptional hospitality in March, this year.\nHe has great vision. He is a strong global voice on concern, especially for small island countries.\nExcellency, just as we appreciate your country's beauty\nLet me repeat what I said in Seychelles: Your country shows that size is no barrier to the scale of achievements\nPresident Michel has also been instrumental in our relationship. We value this greatly because Seychelles is a strategic partner for India.\nJust as the range of our interests are broad, so are the foundations of our relationship deep - our shared values, ties of kinship, unmatched mutual respect and trust, and a partnership of equality.\nI am happy that we are making good progress on the decisions in March. President Michel's visit has been extended\nWe have excellent security cooperation, especially for maritime security in the Indian Ocean Region.\nWe are honored to be a partner in providing aircraft, naval vessels and coastal radar systems for strengthening surveillance capacities. Our cooperation in hydrography surveys is extensive and growing.\nI have conveyed that India will gift one more Interceptor Coastguard boat to Seychelles\nIn March, I had announced that we would gift a second Dornier aircraft to Seychelles. I am happy that we have completed the process and signed the agreement today.\nPresident and I place strong emphasis on Ocean or Blue Economy. I believe, as he does, that we should look at the small island countries\nWe are pleased that sustainable use of oceans and marine resources is a distinct element in the sustainable development goals in the United Nations that were recently finalized.\nOur bilateral agreement for cooperation on blue economy is a huge step forward in our relationship and promoting the sustainable ocean economy in the region.\nWe look forward to cooperating in Space, including the areas of managing land and marine resources, fisheries advisory, weather forecasting and disaster management.\nWe have begun preparations for infrastructure development on the Assumption Island in Seychelles.\nWe had spoken about improving connectivity The Air Services Agreement will enable more and easier connections between the two countries.\nClimate change is a major global challenge It is a serious threat to island countries like Seychelles With a shoreline of 7500 kilometres and about 1300 islands, India has similar concerns\nWe both have ambitious national plans for a more sustainable future. We also work together for a comprehensive, balanced and effective outcome on climate change at COP 21 in Paris later this year.\nI shared our desires for reforms of the United Nations, including its Security Council, in the 70th anniversary year.\nWe greatly appreciate Seychelles' support for India's candidature for permanent membership of a United Nations Security Council\nI also conveyed our desire to work with Small Island Developing States to realize their vision of Samoa Pathway and to be a source of strength for each other in international forums\nI thank President Michel for his support for the Third India-Africa Forum Summit to be held in October.\nLet me also express our gratitude to Seychelles for their active support in making the International Day of Yoga a big success.\nExcellency, We are two countries of vastly different sizes But, this relationship is a outstanding model of international partnership. This special and unique relationship will always be on the center of our interests.\nI am confident that this milestone year will be our relationship.\nThank you very much"
